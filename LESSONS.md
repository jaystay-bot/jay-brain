# Lessons

## Build
- Prompt 0 (context) prevents AI drift
- One prompt at a time prevents breakage
- Specific instructions = better output

## Product
- Distribution > product quality
- Track record before paywall
- Free + affiliate works best early

## Execution
- Prove data source works before building
- Mock data must be labeled
- Keep infra cheap until revenue

## Monetization Order
1. Subscription
2. Retention (alerts)
3. Affiliate
4. Sharing
5. Annual plans
